Welcome to the official website of the Houston Rockets, a professional basketball team based in Houston, Texas. The Rockets are a member of the Western Conference Southwest Division in the National Basketball Association (NBA). Founded in 1967, the team has a rich history and has won two NBA championships in 1994 and 1995. The Rockets are known for their passionate fan base and their iconic red and white colors.
